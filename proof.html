The lever

The call funnel halves your CAC.

Cheap registrations only pay off if they deposit. Doubling registration-to-FTD conversion from 10% to 20% halves the cost per FTD - and that jump is exactly what a CRM journey plus a timely call delivers.

Without a call funnel

  • Registration cost: $1.5
  • Registration to FTD: 10%

  • Properly built funnels only, no outbound contact

With CRM + call funnel / AI

  • Registration cost: $1.5
  • Registration to FTD: 20%
  • Cost per FTD: $7.5
  • Outbound call assists KYC and the first deposit while intent is hot
