Retail layer

Retail on one operator balance.

The operator funds one prepaid balance. Every bet, payout and player top-up across all halls and points debits or credits it in real time. Begin with a mobile cashier or POS, then scale to dozens of points or a franchise - all the way to a full online product with the same mechanics.

Single prepaid balanceReal-time debit / creditTITO on every pointAnonymous or verified (by jurisdiction)Mobile cashier to franchise
01

Cashier

Staffed point on PC, mobile or POS. Fast, secure payments, deposits and payouts, with TITO voucher codes - the lightest way to start.

02

Betplace

Self-service betting PC for customers, with no bill or coin acceptor, so you can deploy dozens per hall at peak traffic. Bets and TITO vouchers; cash is settled at the cashier.

03

SSBT terminal

Full self-service: bill and coin acceptor, barcode scanner and ticket printer, 24/7. Cash and cashless, with TITO in and out.
